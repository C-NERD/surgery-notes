
- # Septicaemia
	- **The prolonged presence of bacteria in the blood accompanied by a severe systemic reaction.** Implies active multiplication of organisms in the bloodstream — distinct from the transient invasion of [[Bacteraemia]].
		- Source: Baja Ch. 2 (Septicaemia / Sepsis section)
	- Among infectious-disease specialists the term is now largely **superseded by [[Sepsis]]**.
		- Source: Baja Ch. 2 (same section)
	- Related: [[Bacteraemia]], [[Sepsis]], [[Shock]], [[Systemic Inflammatory Response Syndrome]]
