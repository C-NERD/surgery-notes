
- # Colonization
	- **The presence and multiplication of microorganisms on or within host tissue without invoking a local or systemic immune response.** The host is asymptomatic — colonization is *not* infection.
		- Source: Baja Ch. 2 p. 12 (Establishment of Infection); Surgical Infections (UDUTH lecture, Musa) — Definition of Terms
	- Lies on the **wound bioburden spectrum**:
		- contamination → **colonization** → critical colonization → [[Inflammation|infection]]
		- Source: Surgical Infections lecture, slide 10
	- Related: [[Inflammation]], [[Bacteraemia]], [[Sepsis]]
